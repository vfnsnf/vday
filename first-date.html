Our First Date

It was during Navratri when I really noticed you—wearing those yellow traditionals, looking absolutely radiant. I had seen you before, of course, back in school. I still remember your braids, your smile, and the way you carried yourself. Even back then, there was something about you that stood out.

I still remember riding my scooty all the way to Daman just to see you, even if it was only for two minutes and in the middle of the rain. You were that special to me—worth every kilometer, every drop, every second. As I said it was the best day of that year.

We were texting all night non-stop, and I couldn’t get enough of it—I was so genuinely interested in you. Every reply from you made me smile, and I found myself waiting for the next message like it was the best part of my day. It wasn’t just small talk; it felt easy, real, and exciting. I didn’t even notice the time passing because I was so caught up in getting to know you.

Later, watching you through Instagram stories, I honestly thought you were out of my league. You seemed so effortless, confident, and beautiful in a way that felt unreachable to me. I never imagined that one day we’d actually have a story of our own.

But somehow, life brought us together. And even now, I still have moments where I look at you and think, "How did I get so lucky?" What started as distant admiration turned into something real something I get to hold close every day.
